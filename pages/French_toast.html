French Toast

Tasty Tasty in your tummy :D

You want to less and get full?then try this toast to fill your tummy!

What you'll need:

  • Bread
  • Milk
  • Egg
  • Butter
  • Sugar
  • Salt
  • Maple syrup (optional)

Directions:

  1. In a Bowl add Milk,Egg and whisk
  2. Add Sugar and Salt according to your taste(For sweet toast add more sugar, for savoury add less sugar)
  3. Butter your pan
  4. Dip bread and toast it on the pan
  5. Toast until golden-brown is achieved
  6. Plate it add butter on top
  7. Add Maple Syrup (optional)
  8. Enjoy!
