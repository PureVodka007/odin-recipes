Curd Rice

A easy comfort meal

Curd rice is a very simple dish to prepare and it a perfect meal during hot Summer days.

What you'll need:

  • Rice(cold)
  • Curd
  • Mustard seeds
  • Curry leaves
  • Shallots(chopped finely)
  • Oil
  • Dry red chillies

Directions:

  1. Mix Curd with cold rice
  2. Add water if necessary
  3. Add Salt per your taste
  4. In a pan add Oil
  5. Add Mustard Seeds
  6. After they start popping,add Curry leaves
  7. Then add Shallots, dry chillies
  8. Finally pour the hot oil mixture over curd and rice
  9. Done! Enjoy as it is or refrigerate and serve
