Boiled Egg

A lazy way to get daily Protein

Do you need to reach your daily protein goal? or you like to eat raw egg (Are you okay?), Follow this recipe for a Perfect Boiled egg!

What you'll need:

  • Raw Egg(As many you like)
  • Water for boiling
  • Salt and Pepper for taste

Directions:

  1. Pour water in a deep pan and heat it
  2. Wait for the water to boil and add eggs
  3. Wait for 7 mins for Perfect egg else wait more for hard-boiled(9 mins max)
  4. Take egg out and put it in a ice bath
  5. Deshell eggs, cut it, add salt and pepper
  6. Enjoy!
